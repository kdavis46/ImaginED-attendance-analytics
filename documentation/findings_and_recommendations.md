# Findings & Recommendations

## Key Findings

- Attendance rate measured approximately 51%
- Absence rate measured approximately 49%
- Average engagement score measured 2.92
- Engagement and attendance relationships varied across educational programs
- Several schools demonstrated elevated absence concentrations

## Recommendations

### Improve Attendance Outreach
Implement targeted outreach strategies for schools with elevated absence levels.

### Monitor Program Performance
Expand engagement tracking across programs with lower attendance-to-engagement relationships.

### Increase Reporting Visibility
Provide recurring attendance and engagement reporting to leadership stakeholders for operational planning.
