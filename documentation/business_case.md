# Business Case

## Problem Statement

Educational and nonprofit organizations often struggle to measure whether programs are effectively improving attendance and engagement outcomes.

Without centralized reporting, leadership lacks visibility into:
- attendance trends
- absence rates
- engagement performance
- school participation
- program effectiveness

## Objective

Develop a centralized dashboard capable of tracking attendance and engagement metrics across multiple schools and educational support programs.

## Solution

A Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze:
- attendance distribution
- engagement scores
- attendance status trends
- school-level participation
- program effectiveness relationships
